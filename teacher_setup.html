Setup for Teachers

Once an administrator has set up yourschool.littleredbrick.com, teachers can go there and log in. If the administator has not already set up their classes, teachers will have two tasks to complete:

Identify subjects
The first time a teacher logs in, he is directed to a form in which he can select what classes he is teaching in different periods.
Enroll students
Once the teaching load has been set, a teacher must enter the names of all students in his classes, either by typing them in or uploading them from a spreadsheet.

Once students have been enrolled in classes, the setup process is complete, and a teacher may start using the gradebook.

Note

A teacher who signs up for a free individual account rather than as part of a school will be responsible for all the administrative setup tasks in addition to the normal teacher duties.
