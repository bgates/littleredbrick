Why LittleRedBrick?

Gradebooks can do more than just report individual student grades. LittleRedBrick takes the gradebook data teachers already collect, and organizes it to let teachers and administrators quickly understand class performance.

LittleRedBrick lets you ask questions like:

  • Does the chemistry class do better on tests or labs?
  • Which Civics class gives the most weight to homework?
  • Do all the geometry classes have the same distribution of grades?
  • Does a given student do better in classes that emphasize classwork, or in classes that emphasize tests?
  • Do a particular teacher's Spanish classes get more homework than average?
  • When did a student start having trouble in math?

Standard Gradebook Stuff

When teachers enter assignments, they automatically appear on student calendars. When teachers enter scores, they are automatically factored into student cumulative grades. Different permission levels ensure that administrators, teachers, students, and parents each see only what they should.

Extra Features

Online discussion groups to promote communication within every segment of your school community. Multiple levels of reporting allow you to compare multiple sections at once, view one section in detail, or concentrate on a single student in a class. An assignment filter that lets you see how one student or an entire class performs on certain types of assignments (focus on tests, everything due in a certain time period, etc).

Easy to Use

Every page has a link to information on that page's contents and its purpose. Many pages also have tips or instructions embedded in them. There is also a help forum to ask questions to tech support and read how other people had their problems resolved.

No Hardware to Buy, No Software to Download

Just use your internet browser (Internet Explorer 6 or 7 or Firefox). You can log in from school or home, and you always have access to the latest version of the service.
