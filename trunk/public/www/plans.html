Sign Up - Two Options

Plan 1: One Teacher, No Cost

If you are a teacher and you would like to try LittleRedBrick for your own classes, you can sign up for free. If other teachers want to join you later on, you can transition to a paid account for your school at any time.

Plan 2: $10 per teacher, per month

When you sign up with LittleRedBrick, you pay for nothing but teacher account subscriptions. There are no sign-up fees, no termination fees, no long-term contracts, and you can create an unlimited number of administrator, student, and parent accounts for free.

You Pay

  • $10 per teacher per month

You Get

  • Gradebooks for every teacher
  • Class and student performance reports
  • Discussion groups
  • Calendars for every user

There is No Charge For

  • Signing up
  • Creating an individual teacher account
  • Canceling at any time
  • Unlimited administrator accounts
  • Unlimited student accounts
  • Unlimited parent accounts

What kind of computer do I need?

If you can read this page, you are probably in good shape. Any of the following web browsers work with LittleRedBrick: IE 6, 7, and 8 (PC), Firefox 2, 3, 3.5 (PC, or Linux), and Safari 4 (tested on PC)

What if I decide to cancel?

No problem. LittleRedBrick is billed monthly, and you can cancel at any time. If you would like to keep copies of any of your data, let us know and we will send it to you.

How do I pay for it?

We're flexible, and we understand your school or district may not be. Go ahead and create an account and start using it, and we'll contact you to work out payment.
